Laboratory Mix Design Studies

Project-specific mix design studies for ground engineering excellence

What We Offer

CE Geotech provides tailored laboratory mix design studies to meet your project’s technical and performance requirements. Our laboratory team delivers comprehensive characterisation and optimisation of hydraulically bound materials for ground engineering.

Materials Characterisation Includes:

  • Optimum Moisture Content & Maximum Dry Density (OMC-MDD) – BS EN 13286-4:2003, BS 1377-4:1990
  • Moisture Condition Value (MCV Range) – BS EN 13286-46:2003
  • BRE-SD1 Sulphate Suite
  • Particle Size Distribution (PSD) Wet/Dry – BS 1377-2:1990
  • Plasticity Index – BS 1377-2:1990
  • Lime Demand – BS 1924-2 Clause 5.4:2018

Assessment of Strength/Stiffness:

  • California Bearing Ratio (CBR) and Immediate Bearing Index (IBI) – BS EN 13286-47:2012, BS 1377-4:1990
  • CBR Swell - BS EN 13286-47:2012, BS 1377-4:1990
  • Accelerated Swell Testing BS EN 13286-49:2004
  • Loss of Strength on Immersion SHW Series 800
  • Unconfined Compressive Strength - BS EN 13286-41:2004
  • Elastic Modulus - BS EN 13286-43: 2003
  • Quick Undrained Triaxial – BS 1377-7:1990
  • Permeability (Falling Head) - K H Head:2011
